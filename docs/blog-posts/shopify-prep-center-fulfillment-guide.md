## What is a Shopify Prep Center? (200 words)

### Definition
A Shopify prep center is a third-party fulfillment service that integrates with your Shopify store to handle:
- Order receiving and processing
- Inventory management
- Pick and pack operations
- Branded packaging and inserts
- Shipping and tracking

### How It Works
1. **Integration:** Connect prep center to Shopify store
2. **Inventory:** Ship products to prep center warehouse
3. **Orders:** Customers place orders on your Shopify store
4. **Fulfillment:** Prep center receives order, picks/packs, ships
5. **Tracking:** Customer receives tracking automatically

### Shopify Prep vs. Fulfillment Center
- **Both handle:** Order fulfillment, inventory, shipping
- **Prep center focus:** Product preparation, compliance, branding
- **Best for DTC:** Shopify stores with custom branding needs

## How Shopify Integration Works (200 words)

### Technical Integration
Most Shopify prep centers use:
- **Shopify API:** Direct integration with your store
- **Automatic syncing:** Orders flow from Shopify to prep center
- **Real-time inventory:** Updated automatically
- **Tracking updates:** Sent back to Shopify and customers

### Setup Process
1. **Choose prep center:** Research and select provider
2. **Initial setup:** Grant API access to prep center
3. **Product mapping:** Map SKUs to physical inventory
4. **Shipping rules:** Configure carrier preferences
5. **Test orders:** Run test orders to ensure accuracy
6. **Go live:** Start fulfilling real orders

### Ongoing Management
- **Monitor inventory:** Check levels via dashboard
- **Review performance:** Track order accuracy and speed
- **Adjust settings:** Update products, pricing, shipping rules
- **Customer support:** Handle customer inquiries about orders

## Cost Breakdown (150 words)

### Typical Shopify Prep Center Pricing

Most Shopify prep centers offer volume-based pricing with transparent fee structures. Pricing typically includes:

**Receiving & Storage**
- Per-unit receiving fees
- Monthly storage (by pallet or cubic footage)

**Order Fulfillment**
- Per-order pick & pack fees
- Additional item fees for multi-item orders

**Value-Added Services**
- Kitting and bundling services
- Custom packaging and branded materials

**Monthly Cost Example**
For a store processing 500 orders/month with custom branded packaging and average 1.5 items per order, costs typically range from $1,500-2,500/month, which compares favorably to DIY fulfillment costs (warehouse rent + labor + supplies = $3,000-5,000/month).

Most prep centers offer custom quotes based on your specific volume and service needs. Contact providers directly for accurate pricing tailored to your business.

## Common Shopify Fulfillment Questions (150 words)

### How long does setup take?
Typically 1-2 weeks from initial contact to first order fulfilled.

### Can I use multiple prep centers?
Yes, many brands use regional centers for faster shipping.

### What if I already have inventory?
Most prep centers will receive your existing inventory from your current location.

### Can prep centers handle returns?
Yes, most offer return management and restocking services.

### Do I need to use all fulfillment services?
No, you can pick and pack yourself and just use storage, or vice versa.
