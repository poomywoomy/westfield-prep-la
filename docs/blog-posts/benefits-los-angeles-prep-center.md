## Benefit #1: Port Proximity = Faster Receiving (250 words)

### LA/Long Beach Ports
- Largest port complex in the Western Hemisphere
- Handles 40% of US imports
- Direct access to international shipments

### Time Savings
- **Traditional route:** Port → Eastern warehouse → 7-10 days
- **LA prep center route:** Port → Local prep → 1-2 days
- **Result:** 5-8 days faster to market

### Cost Savings
- Lower drayage fees (short-distance trucking from port)
- Reduced inland transportation costs
- Avoid long-haul shipping delays

### Real-World Example
"A Shopify seller importing from China can have products prepped and shipped to customers within 3 days of arrival at Long Beach port—compared to 10-14 days with an East Coast warehouse."

### Best For:
- International sellers (China, Vietnam, Thailand)
- High-volume importers
- Businesses with frequent container shipments
