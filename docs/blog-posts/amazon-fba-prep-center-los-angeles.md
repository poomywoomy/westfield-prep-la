## Amazon FBA Prep Requirements (250 words)

### What is Amazon FBA Prep?
Preparing products to meet Amazon's strict fulfillment requirements before sending to Amazon warehouses.

### Essential FBA Prep Services

**1. FNSKU Labeling**
- What: Amazon's unique product identifier
- Why: Tracks inventory in Amazon system
- Requirement: Every unit must have FNSKU label
- **Common violation:** Missing or incorrect FNSKU

**2. Polybagging**
- What: Clear plastic bags with suffocation warning
- When: Products with fabric, openable packaging, or exposed parts
- Requirement: Suffocation warning label (in specific size/format)
- **Common violation:** Missing warning labels

**3. Bubble Wrap & Packaging**
- What: Protective packaging for fragile items
- When: Glass, electronics, delicate items
- Requirement: Prevent damage during shipping
- **Common violation:** Insufficient protection

**4. Carton Prep**
- What: Case packing with FBA box labels
- Why: Amazon needs to identify shipment contents
- Requirement: Proper labeling, weight limits, size restrictions
- **Common violation:** Overweight boxes, missing labels

**5. Expiration Date Labeling**
- What: Clear expiration dates on food, supplements, cosmetics
- When: Any product with shelf life
- Requirement: Format YYYY-MM-DD, visible on each unit
- **Common violation:** Incorrect format, illegible dates
