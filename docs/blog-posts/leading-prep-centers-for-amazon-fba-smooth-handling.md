### What Are Prep Centers for Amazon FBA

Prep centers are third-party logistics providers that prepare your inventory according to Amazon's strict requirements. These services include:

- FNSKU labeling
- Polybagging and bundling
- Quality inspection
- Shipment creation
- Inventory storage and forwarding

Instead of handling these tasks yourself, prep centers ensure that your products are Amazon-ready without delays or compliance issues.

### Why Sellers Choose Prep Centers

Managing fulfillment in-house may seem cost-effective initially, but it quickly becomes overwhelming as order volume increases. Here's why sellers rely on prep centers for Amazon FBA:

#### 1. Faster Processing

Top prep centers offer 24-hour turnaround times, ensuring inventory reaches Amazon warehouses quickly and starts selling sooner.

#### 2. Error Reduction

Mistakes like incorrect labeling or packaging can lead to Amazon penalties. Professional prep centers maintain high accuracy rates, often above 99%.

#### 3. Scalability

Whether you're shipping 50 units or 50,000, prep centers scale with your business, making them ideal as a 3pl for startups.

#### 4. Cost Efficiency

Outsourcing eliminates the need for warehouse space, staff, and equipment, helping reduce overhead costs.
