## 📦 What is a Prep Center and Why Does It Matter?

A prep center is a professional service that handles product inspection, labeling, packaging, and bundling before your items ship to Amazon fulfillment centers. Think of it as your behind the scenes operations team.

Amazon has strict requirements for how products must be prepared. Miss a label? Wrong poly bag size? Expect rejected shipments, delays, and even account suspensions. A prep center for Amazon FBA takes care of all that so you do not have to worry about compliance headaches.

**Here is what a typical prep center handles:**

- Product inspection and quality checks
- FNSKU labeling and barcode application
- Poly bagging and shrink wrapping
- Bundle creation and kitting
- Box preparation and shipment planning
- Compliance with Amazon's ever changing guidelines

According to [Jungle Scout's 2024 State of the Seller report](https://www.junglescout.com/resources/articles/amazon-seller-statistics/), over 60% of Amazon sellers now use third party services for at least part of their fulfillment process. That number keeps rising because sellers who outsource prep scale faster and make fewer costly mistakes.
