## What is Shopify Order Fulfillment?

Shopify order fulfillment is the complete process of getting products from your warehouse shelves into your customers' hands. It sounds simple, but the execution separates six figure stores from seven figure empires.

**The 6 step fulfillment cycle:**

1. **Order received** via your Shopify store
2. **Inventory allocated** from available stock
3. **Picking** products from warehouse shelves
4. **Packing** items securely with branded materials
5. **Shipping** via optimal carrier routes
6. **Delivery** to your customer's doorstep

In 2026, customers expect 2 day delivery as standard. Anything slower feels like an eternity. Your fulfillment speed directly impacts conversion rates, repeat purchases, and reviews.

## Three Fulfillment Options for Shopify Sellers

Every Shopify merchant faces the same decision: who handles fulfillment? Your three main options each come with distinct tradeoffs.

### Option 1: In House Fulfillment

You pack and ship everything yourself or hire staff to do it from your own space.

**Best for:** Brands with primarily local customers, those offering in store pickup, or sellers with highly customized products requiring hands on assembly.

**The reality:** This approach works until order volume hits around 50 to 100 orders per day. Beyond that, you're spending more time on logistics than growing your business. The hidden costs of warehouse space, labor, packaging supplies, and software add up quickly.

### Option 2: Dropshipping

Your manufacturer or supplier ships directly to customers. You never touch the product.

**Best for:** Testing new product ideas with minimal investment.

**The reality:** Dropshipping sounds attractive until you realize the drawbacks. Transit times often stretch to 2 to 4 weeks when products ship from overseas. Quality control is nonexistent since your customer sees the product before you do. Returns become nightmares. And profit margins shrink because suppliers charge premium rates.

### Option 3: Third Party Logistics (3PL)

A specialized fulfillment partner stores your inventory and handles picking, packing, and shipping on your behalf.

**Best for:** Growing brands that want professional logistics without building their own infrastructure.

**The reality:** This is where most successful Shopify stores land. You get warehouse expertise, carrier discounts, and technology integration without the capital investment. The right [Shopify fulfillment services](/shopify-fulfillment) partner becomes an extension of your team.

## Shopify Fulfillment Network (SFN): An Honest Review for 2026

Shopify launched their own fulfillment solution called Shopify Fulfillment Network in 2019. Here's what you need to know before signing up.

**What SFN offers:**

- Inventory storage across US fulfillment centers
- Picking, packing, and shipping for Shopify orders
- Integration directly within your Shopify admin
- Partnerships with USPS, DHL, UPS, and regional carriers

**Eligibility requirements:**

- Must sell to US customers
- Active Shopify Payments account required
- Must ship between 10 and 200 orders per day
- Cannot exceed 2,000 SKUs
- Products must pass eligibility screening

**SFN limitations to consider:**

- **US only** with no international fulfillment centers
- **Product restrictions** including no beauty items, medical products, perishables, CBD, or hand sanitizers
- **No lot tracking** for products requiring batch or expiration management
- **Unclear onboarding timeline** compared to independent 3PLs

SFN works well for straightforward Shopify stores selling standard consumer products domestically. But if you need international reach, specialized product handling, or rapid scaling capacity, an independent 3PL offers more flexibility.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is Shopify order fulfillment?

Shopify order fulfillment encompasses the entire process of receiving customer orders, picking products from inventory, packing items securely, and shipping packages to buyers. It includes inventory management, carrier selection, tracking updates, and returns processing. Effective fulfillment ensures orders reach customers quickly and accurately while maintaining healthy profit margins.

### How do I set up fulfillment on Shopify in 2026?

Setting up fulfillment on Shopify involves choosing a fulfillment method (self fulfillment, dropshipping, or 3PL), installing the appropriate apps or integrations, configuring shipping rates, and establishing inventory locations. For 3PL partnerships, you install the provider's app, authorize the connection, ship inventory to their warehouse, and orders automatically route for fulfillment once products are received.

### What is the difference between SFN and a 3PL?

Shopify Fulfillment Network (SFN) is Shopify's proprietary fulfillment service limited to US operations with specific product and volume requirements. Independent 3PLs offer broader geographic coverage including international fulfillment, accept wider product ranges, support various order volumes from startup to enterprise, and often provide faster onboarding with more transparent pricing structures.

### How much does Shopify fulfillment cost per order?

Shopify fulfillment costs typically range from $4 to $8 per order depending on item count, product size, shipping destination, and service level. This includes pick and pack fees ($2.50 to $4.00 for first item), packaging materials, and handling. Monthly storage ($25 to $45 per pallet) and receiving fees ($0.25 to $0.50 per unit) add to total fulfillment spend.

### Can I use a 3PL with Shopify Plus?

Yes, Shopify Plus works seamlessly with third party logistics providers. Most established 3PLs offer certified Shopify Plus integrations that support enterprise features including multiple storefronts, B2B wholesale channels, automation workflows, and advanced reporting. These integrations provide real time inventory sync and order routing across all connected stores.

### How fast should Shopify orders be fulfilled in 2026?

Customer expectations in 2026 demand same day or next day fulfillment for orders placed before daily cutoff times. Orders should ship within 24 hours of receipt at maximum. Two day delivery has become the baseline expectation for most products, with premium shipping options offering next day or even same day delivery in major metropolitan areas.

### What products cannot use Shopify Fulfillment Network?

SFN prohibits numerous product categories including beauty and cosmetics, medical devices, perishable goods, products requiring temperature control, CBD and hemp items, hand sanitizers, hazardous materials, and items requiring lot or batch tracking. Products requiring special certifications or regulatory compliance may also be ineligible. Check Shopify's complete prohibited items list before applying.

### Is a Los Angeles 3PL better for West Coast customers?

Absolutely. Fulfilling from Los Angeles provides significant advantages for West Coast customers including next day ground delivery across California, 2 day ground to most Western states, and reduced shipping costs due to shorter zone distances. With 50 million people within 2 day ground shipping range, LA fulfillment serves a massive population efficiently while maintaining fast transit times to the rest of the country.
