## What is a Prep Center? (200 words)

### Core Definition
- Third-party logistics service specializing in e-commerce preparation
- Handles receiving, inspection, labeling, packaging, and shipping
- Focus on compliance (Amazon FBA, Shopify, TikTok Shop)

### Primary Functions
- **Receiving:** Accept shipments from manufacturers/suppliers
- **Quality Control:** Inspect products for damage or defects
- **Labeling:** Apply FNSKU, UPC, or custom labels
- **Packaging:** Polybagging, bubble wrap, custom branding
- **Shipping:** Forward to Amazon FCs or direct-to-consumer

### Who Uses Prep Centers?
- Amazon FBA sellers
- Shopify/DTC brands
- Multi-channel e-commerce businesses
- International sellers shipping to US markets
- Sellers without warehouse space

## Prep Center vs. Fulfillment Center vs. 3PL (250 words)

### Prep Center
- **Focus:** Product preparation & compliance
- **Best for:** Amazon FBA sellers, product prep before FBA
- **Services:** Receiving, labeling, packaging, FBA forwarding
- **Example:** Receiving imported goods, labeling FNSKU, sending to Amazon

### Fulfillment Center (3PL)
- **Focus:** Full order fulfillment & warehousing
- **Best for:** Shopify stores, multi-channel DTC brands
- **Services:** Inventory storage, pick/pack, shipping, returns
- **Example:** Storing 10,000 units, picking orders, shipping to customers

### Key Differences
| Feature | Prep Center | Fulfillment Center |
|---------|-------------|-------------------|
| Storage | Short-term | Long-term |
| Primary Focus | Compliance prep | Order fulfillment |
| Shipping | To Amazon FCs | To end customers |
| Typical Client | FBA sellers | DTC brands |

### When to Use Each
- **Use a prep center if:** You're an Amazon FBA seller who needs product prep and compliance
- **Use a fulfillment center if:** You're a Shopify/DTC brand shipping directly to customers
- **Use both if:** You sell on both Amazon and your own Shopify store

## Common Prep Center Questions (150 words)

### How much does a prep center cost?
Prep center costs vary based on volume and services needed. Most prep centers charge per-unit fees for services like receiving, labeling, and packaging, with volume discounts available for larger accounts. Storage is typically charged by pallet or cubic footage on a monthly basis. The more you ship, the better your per-unit rates become. Contact prep centers directly for custom quotes based on your specific volume and service requirements.

### What's the turnaround time?
Most prep centers offer 1-3 day turnaround. Premium services like Westfield offer same-day processing for orders received before 2 PM.

### Do I need a prep center for Amazon FBA?
Not required, but highly recommended for compliance, quality control, and time savings.

### Can prep centers handle Shopify orders?
Many prep centers offer both FBA prep and DTC fulfillment. Check if they support multi-channel selling.
